Grumpy Cat Teams Up With Purina And Petsmart At Her 'Save A Fortune' Birthday Party In NYC
Cat Events

America's most beloved sourpuss - the one and only Grumpy Cat - recently turned six and we helped her celebrate with Purina® and PetSmart's® "Save A Fortune" event in New York City! There were balloons, cake, a fortune teller, and yes, Grumpy Cat was on site to begrudgingly pose for pictures with her adoring fans. Grumpy's one birthday wish is to help her fur-riends Save A Fortune this year - so read on for more birthday party pics and details!

DISCLOSURE: This post has been compensated by Collective Bias, Inc. and its advertiser. All opinions are mine alone. #FortunaForAll #CollectiveBias #CBias
Grumpy Cat teamed up with Purina® and PetSmart® to celebrate her sixth birthday party here in NYC! When Grumpy Cat invites you to her party, clearly you don't say no - so we were there to sing her happy birthday and watch her blow out her candles last week:

Contrary to her frowny appearance, Grumpy Cat's only birthday wish is for all of her pet friends to have a "pros-purr-ous year" so she has teamed up with Petsmart and Purina for their "Save A Fortune" promotion. I'm not gonna lie: Grumpy Cat's birthday party was way better than my last birthday party. There was a cake and cake pops, Grumpy Cat balloons, limited edition Grumpy Cat tarot cards, and a local fortune teller who read my fortune for me (spoiler: she said the next 3 months for me involves a lot of writing .... but that it would all be beneficial to my larger goals! Yay!)

Grumpy Cat is so generous that consumers who spend $20 at Petsmart on qualifying Purina products (both dog and cat products) in a single purchase will receive a $5 gift card (mailed within 8-10 weeks) after submitting a photo of the receipt of purchase. Pet owners can also get additional gift cards for each additional $20 they spend. The promo runs through May 18 for purchases, and you can redeem your gift card online until May 31. And shoppers can save even more by going online and participating in an interactive "instant win" game with clairvoyant fortune telling kitty, Madame Fortuna - all at PurinaFortunes.com!

Consumers can also enter the "Paws of Fate" Sweepstakes now through May 31 for the chance to win one of two grand prizes - the Dream of Fortunes Pet Beds (one for a cat, one for a dog). Enter by registering for the program at PurinaFortunes.com and share on Twitter for additional bonus entries. Check out the Social Hub to access the Rebate/Instant Win site.

We recently made a trip to PetSmart to stock up on some of our favorite Thomas-approved products:

We picked up a new dry food, Purina® Pro Plan® SAVOR® Shredded Blend in chicken and rice (you know how Thomas loves his chicken!) - it contains real poultry as the #1 ingredient, as well as omega fatty acids to keep Thomas's skin and coat looking supa fly. Look how he can't wait to get in the bag:

We also picked up some Tidy Cats Free and Clean Unscented Litter. It's super lightweight and keeps Thomas's toilet smelling fresh (well, as fresh as possible for a litter box!) We like it because it's free of dyes and contains natural activated charcoal (which - if you don't know - sucks up gross odors like nothing else!)

So it turns out Grumpy Cat only has a grumpy exterior and a super sweet inside - hurry and register for your PetSmart gift cards and take advantage of Grumpy's "Save A Fortune" promo going on right now at PurinaFortunes.com. On that note, we will leave you with one of our fave pics from Grumpy Cat's birthday party. Til next time!
