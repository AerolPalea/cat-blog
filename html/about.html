About

That Cat Blog is based in Brooklyn, NY and showcases
cat-related stories in the news and pop culture.

TCB is run by a lady named Patty Scull, who has embraced her oddness and her total obsession with cats. Her writing has appeared in: Brooklyn Vegan, Greenpointers, Short and Sweet NYC, Broke Ass Stuart's Goddamn Website, Stepaway Magazine, Erma Bombeck's HumorWriters.org, and other various places around the web.

Patty is a Professional Member of the Cat Writer's Association.

Thomas the cat's writing has appeared in this blog, where he hopes to launch his superstar writing career reviewing things like catnip, cat toys, and proteins in different flavors.

To submit pitches or just to say “howdy!” please fill out our contact form.
